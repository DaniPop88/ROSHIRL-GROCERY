Privacy Policy
At Roshirl Ramirez Grocery Hub, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the details you share with us, so you can shop with confidence.
1. Information We Collect
We collect only the information necessary to process your orders and provide excellent service:
  • Name, address, and contact details for delivery and communication
  • Order history and preferences for improving your experience
  • Payment information for transactions (securely processed via trusted payment partners)
2. How We Use Your Data
Your information is used exclusively for:
  • Fulfilling your orders and providing updates
  • Responding to inquiries and customer support
  • Offering promotions or updates with your permission
  • Improving our products, services, and website
3. Data Protection & Security
We take data security seriously. All personal information is stored safely and only accessible to authorized team members. Our payment process uses secure, encrypted channels.
4. Sharing Information
Roshirl Ramirez Grocery Hub does not sell or share your personal data with outside parties, except when required by law or for delivery and payment processing. Our partners are selected for their reliability and commitment to privacy.
5. Your Choices & Rights
You may request to view, update, or delete your personal information at any time. For questions or concerns about your privacy, please contact us at info@roshirlramirezgrocery.com.
6. Changes to Our Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Any changes will be posted here for your review. We encourage you to check this page periodically.
Thank you for trusting Roshirl Ramirez Grocery Hub. Your privacy matters to us.
